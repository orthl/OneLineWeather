About One Line Weather

Weather Information, Simplified

One Line Weather is more than just another weather app. It’s a tool designed for simplicity, clarity, and speed — built for people who value quick and distraction-free access to weather data. In a world of overloaded apps, cookie banners and excessive tracking, One Line Weather delivers exactly what you need: the current weather in a single, easy-to-read sentence.

Fast, Clean and Minimalist Forecasts

The app provides instant weather summaries. As soon as you open the page, you’ll see the current temperature, the dominant condition, and a comparison to yesterday — all in one concise sentence. This makes it perfect for a quick glance in the morning, on the go, or on minimalist displays like TRMNL or E-Ink devices.

Detailed Daily Outlooks

Beneath the main sentence, the app shows weather details for morning, noon, evening, and night — including average temperatures and rain probabilities. All data comes from trusted, publicly available sources to ensure global accuracy and transparency.

Privacy-First Weather App

One Line Weather collects no personal data. There are no cookies, no tracking scripts, and no analytics. Your location is processed anonymously for forecast delivery, using open and privacy-respecting technologies such as OpenStreetMap’s Nominatim and Open-Meteo.

Open Source and Transparent

One Line Weather is fully open source. The project is available for anyone to inspect, adapt, or contribute to. This transparency ensures long-term trust and allows developers to use or host the application independently — whether for personal projects, self-hosted dashboards or lightweight weather displays. Open code, open data, and a commitment to public infrastructure are at the core of the app’s design.

Designed for Minimalism and Speed

The app’s interface is deliberately reduced to the essentials. It loads fast and runs smoothly, even on older or low-powered hardware. Whether you're using a smartphone, tablet, desktop, or a low-power E-Ink screen, One Line Weather delivers a consistent and clear experience.

For Minimalists, Makers and Professionals

This app is ideal for anyone tired of bloated weather apps. It appeals to digital minimalists, privacy-conscious users, developers, designers, and professionals who need quick weather insights. The typographic clarity also makes it well-suited for office dashboards or info displays.

Future-Ready: Local AI and More

One Line Weather is in active development. Features like TRMNL integration, on-device AI-generated weather descriptions using transformers.js, and weekly trend summaries are planned. But the mission will always stay the same: clear, helpful weather data without noise or compromise.

About One Line Weather

One Line Weather is a simple and focused weather app that shows the current conditions in one sentence — no ads, no tracking, no distractions. Designed for speed and clarity, it's the perfect tool for those who just want to know what the weather is like, instantly.

Why One Line Weather?

While most weather apps overwhelm users with data, images, and trackers, One Line Weather does the opposite. It’s fast, clean, and entirely focused on the essentials: current temperature, condition, and a daily comparison to yesterday — all displayed in a clear, readable sentence.

Below the summary, you’ll find a visual forecast for morning, noon, evening, and night, including average temperature and rain probability per period.

Privacy First

One Line Weather collects no personal data. No cookies, no analytics, no third-party tracking. All geolocation data is handled locally, using only public, privacy-respecting APIs like OpenStreetMap’s Nominatim and Open-Meteo.

Made for Simplicity

This app is ideal for anyone who prefers reduced digital interfaces — from mobile minimalists to E-Ink display users and TRMNL fans. It’s fully responsive and optimized for both desktop and low-power devices.

Technology Stack

  • 🌍 Geolocation via OpenStreetMap Nominatim
  • 🌤 Weather data from Open-Meteo
  • 🖼️ Icons by Meteocons by Bas
  • 🔤 Font: FiraCode

Roadmap

We're working on new features like TRMNL integration, local AI-generated weather sentences (on-device), and weekly trends. The goal remains the same: clarity and control.

Who is this for?

Whether you’re a developer, designer, digital minimalist, or just want the weather without the noise — One Line Weather is made for you.
